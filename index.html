Cats Breed

SIAMESE

Known for their striking blue eyes and sleek, slender bodies. They are intelligent, vocal, and affectionate, but can be very demanding and sometimes territorial.

PERSIAN

Recognized for their long, thick coats and flat faces. They are typically calm, quiet, and gentle, but can require a lot of grooming and may be prone to health issues.

HIMALAYAN

A breed that is a cross between a Persian and a Siamese. They have a distinctive pointed coat and are known for their affectionate and gentle personalities.

BRITISH SHORTHAIR

A stocky, muscular cat with a dense, plush coat. They are typically easygoing and friendly, but can be somewhat reserved around strangers.
